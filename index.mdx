Space is a planning method — built into an app for Mac and iOS. You can download it from the [App Store](https://apps.apple.com/us/app/space-life-planner/id6502294348).
It connects your yearly ambitions to what you do today: zoom out to set long-term goals, then zoom in to make progress on them in your day to day.
