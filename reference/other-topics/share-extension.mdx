Space includes a _share extension_ that allows you to share **to** Space from other apps on Mac and iOS.

## Sharing to Space on the Mac

### Enabling the extension

On Mac, you have to manually enable the Share Extension before you can use it. Open `System Settings` > `General` > `Login Items &amp; Extensions`.
Scroll down to `Extensions` and click `By Category`. Then click on the <Icon icon="circle-info" /> next to `Sharing`.

In the list, find Space and toggle it on.

### Using the extension

In any app that supports Share Extensions, open the Share menu and click Space.

<Frame caption="Open the Share menu and click Space">
  <img src="/images/share/mac-share-1.jpeg" />
</Frame>

You'll then see a popup allowing you to edit how Space will save the task.

<Frame caption="Creating a task in your Inbox">
  <img src="/images/share/mac-share-2.jpeg" />
</Frame>

Sharing to Space on iOS

On iOS things are a little easier since we don't need to enable the extension; it is always on. Just open an app that supports sharing, tap the share icon, and find Space in the list of apps. 
When Space is not shown immediately, tap `More` and look for Space in the resulting list.
