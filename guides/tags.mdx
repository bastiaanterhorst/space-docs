---
title: "Tags"
description: "A flexible way to categorise across tasks, projects and areas"
---

Tags are a flexible way to categorize tasks, projects and areas. Tags are different in that they cut across everything, bringing tasks from all over the app together, as long as they share the same tag. Check out the video for a quick demo of how tags work and how you can use them.
