Space for Mac has a built-in MCP server that lets you integrate Space with agentic AI tools. MCP (Model Context Protocol) is 
emerging as the standard way to connect apps to AI agents, and it is easy to set up. The video below shows you how you can connect
Space to OpenAI Codex and Craft Agents, and setup for other agentic tools will be very similar.
